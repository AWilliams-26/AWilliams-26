    • Background

    Released in 1985, Gradius is a thrilling Shoot-Em-Up series comprised of over ten games! The first game includes seven stages (eight on PC Engine), a variety of power-ups, and encounters that will keep you on your feet! If you enjoy this action-packed game, then the later entries will be sure to keep you engaged!
