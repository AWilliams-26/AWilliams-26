    Gradius is a series known for its challenges, with enemies at every turn and bosses at the end of each stage. Luckily, each fighter is modular and equipped with a system to upgrade their weaponry! Below is a list of some weapons you can use throughout the series.


    Weapons in Gradius

    Double Gun: Shoots a bullet ahead of you and one diagonally.

    Tail Gun: Shoots a bullet ahead of you and one behind you.

    Laser: Shoots a laser straight ahead that does massive damage.

    Ripple Laser: Shoots a laser ring that gets larger as it travels.

    Missile: Shoots a missile that will coast along the ground.

    Spread Bomb: Shoots a missile that will explode on contact with the ground.

    Photon Torpedo: Shoots a missile that will cruise on the ground through multiple enemies.

    Two-Way Missile: Shoots two missiles that will attack enemies both above and below you.
